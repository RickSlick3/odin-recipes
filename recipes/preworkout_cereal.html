Preworkout Cereal

This gigantic bowl of cereal will surely get your carbed up and full of energy. This is a meal suited for anyone who is getting ready to lift some heavy weights.

This recipe is quick and easy, and will give you the proportionate macros for your workouts.

Not satisfied, double the protein powder for more flavor (and PROTEIN) or add more cereal or banana to meet your macros

What You'll Need:

  • Ingredients
    • 2.5 servings (100 grams) of Rise Krispies cereal
    • 1 scoop (~32 grams)of your favorite protein powder
    • 1 serving (~33 grams) of peanut butter
    • 1-2 bananas
    • 2.5 cups of your preferred milk (optional)
  • Supplies
    • A shaker bottle
    • A large bowl

Steps to follow:

  1. Pour your cereal into the bowl

    Maybe now you will see why the bowl needs to be large.

  2. Add your banana(s) to the bowl

    Slice the bananas into any sized chunks over the bowl for easy preperation. Peeled, of course.

  3. Add your protein powder, peanut butter, and choice of liquid to the shaker bottle

    This will allow the peanut butter flavor to be distributed evenly in the bowl.

    I like to use water for less calories and more plain flavor.

  4. Shake your shaker bottle well and pour over the cereal

    Shake for a minimum of 15 seconds. Pour and enjoy!
