Monster Mash

Monster Mash is a meal best suited for volume eaters. Comparable to hibachi, this meal is made of large portions of rice, beef, stir-fry veggies, eggs, and your choice of sauce.

Grab the biggest bowl you can find and get ready to prepare this monster sized meal.

What You'll Need:

  • Ingredients
    • 1 pound of pre-cut stir-fry beef
    • 1.5 cups of Minute Rice
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1-2 cups of stirfry veggies
    • Stir-fry sauce of your choice
  • Supplies
    • A medium sized pot with a lid
    • A large pan/wok
    • A spatula
    • Non-stick cooking spray

Steps to follow:

  1. Dump your beef into the pan/wok and turn your stove burner to medium heat

    The beef will need to cook for a while, so leave the stove on while you continue to work on the next steps.

    I recommend stirring the beed with the spatula between each next step.

  2. Pour 1.5 cups of water into your medium sized pot and turn your stove burner to max heat

    The water will boil quickly, so measure out your 1.5 cups of Minute Rice right after

    Once the water is boiling, pour the rice into the pot, cover, and remove from the hot burner.

    Don't forget to turn off the burner!

  3. Add your 3 eggs and stirfry veggies

    When the beef looses its raw color, add these ingreditents and stir around in the pan or wok

    Once the eggs look cooked and the veggies are soft, check to ensure the beef is cooked to a minimum of 140 degrees (farenheit).

  4. Remove from the wok and enjoy!

    Don't forget to add your favorite stir-fry sauce to the mix for extra flavor.
