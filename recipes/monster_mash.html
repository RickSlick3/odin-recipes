Monster Mash

Monster Mash is a meal best suited for bulking. Comparable to hibachi, this meal is made of rice, beef, stir-fry veggies, eggs, and your choice of sauce.

Grab the biggest bowl you can find and get ready to prepare this monster sized meal.

What You'll Need:

  • Ingredients
    • 1 pound of pre-cut stir-fry beef
    • 1.5 cups of Minute Rice
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1-2 cups of stirfry veggies
    • Stir-fry sauce of your choice
  • Supplies
    • A medium pot with a lid
    • A large pan/wok
    • A spatula
    • Non-stick cooking spray

Steps to follow:

  1. Dump your beef into the pan/wok and turn your stove burner to medium heat
